• Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal •

    Ingredients: Rolled oats, almond milk, apple, cinnamon, honey, chopped nuts.

    Recipe: Cook rolled oats with almond milk. Stir in diced apple, cinnamon, and honey. Top with chopped nuts.

  • Berry Smoothie Bowl •

    Ingredients: Mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries), banana, Greek yogurt, almond milk, honey, granola, sliced almonds.

    Recipe: Blend mixed berries, banana, Greek yogurt, and almond milk until smooth. Pour into a bowl and top with granola, sliced almonds, and a drizzle of honey..

  • Banana Chia Seed Pudding •

    Ingredients: Ripe bananas, chia seeds, almond milk, honey, vanilla extract, sliced almonds, fresh berries.

    Recipe: Mash ripe bananas and mix with almond milk, chia seeds, honey, and vanilla extract. Let it sit in the refrigerator overnight. Serve topped with sliced almonds and fresh berries..

  • Greek Yogurt Parfait•

    Ingredients: Greek yogurt, mixed berries, granola, honey.

    Recipe: Layer Greek yogurt, mixed berries, and granola in a glass or bowl. Drizzle with honey.

  • Mango Coconut Chia Pudding •

    Ingredients: Mango, coconut milk, chia seeds, shredded coconut, honey.

    Recipe: Blend mango with coconut milk until smooth. Mix with chia seeds and let it sit in the refrigerator until thickened. Serve topped with shredded coconut and a drizzle of honey.

  • Peach Almond Butter Toast •

    Ingredients: Whole grain bread, almond butter, peach slices, honey, sliced almonds.

    Recipe: Toast whole grain bread. Spread almond butter on toast. Top with peach slices, a drizzle of honey, and sliced almonds.

  • Avocado Toast with Grapefruit•

    Ingredients: Whole grain bread, avocado, grapefruit, lemon juice, chili flakes, salt, pepper.

    Recipe: Toast whole grain bread. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Spread avocado mixture on toast. Top with slices of grapefruit and sprinkle with chili flakes.

  • Watermelon Feta Salad •

    Ingredients: Watermelon, feta cheese, fresh mint leaves, lime juice.

    Recipe: Cut watermelon into cubes and toss with crumbled feta cheese. Drizzle with lime juice and garnish with fresh mint leaves.

  • Pineapple Coconut Smoothie •

    Ingredients: Pineapple, coconut water, banana, spinach, shredded coconut.

    Recipe: Blend pineapple, coconut water, banana, and spinach until smooth. Pour into a glass and sprinkle with shredded coconut.

  • Spinach and Mushroom Omelette •

    Ingredients:Eggs, spinach, mushrooms, onion, bell pepper, cheese (optional), salt, pepper.

    Recipe: Saute chopped spinach, mushrooms, onion, and bell pepper in a pan until tender. Beat eggs with salt and pepper, pour over the vegetables in the pan, and cook until set. Optionally, sprinkle cheese on top before folding the omelette.

  • Vegetable Frittata •

    Ingredients: Eggs, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, bell pepper, onion, garlic, spinach, cheese (optional), salt, pepper.

    Recipe: Saute chopped vegetables and garlic in an oven-safe skillet until tender. Beat eggs with salt and pepper, pour over the vegetables, and cook until the edges start to set. Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake until the frittata is cooked through. Optionally, sprinkle cheese on top before baking.

  • Avocado Toast with Tomatoes •

    Ingredients: Whole grain bread, avocado, cherry tomatoes, lemon juice, salt, pepper, red pepper flakes (optional).

    Recipe: Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Spread avocado mixture on toast. Top with sliced cherry tomatoes and sprinkle with red pepper flakes if desired.

  • Vegetable Breakfast Burrito•

    Ingredients: Whole grain tortillas, scrambled eggs, black beans, bell peppers, onions, avocado, salsa, cheese (optional).

    Recipe: Fill whole grain tortillas with scrambled eggs, black beans, sauteed bell peppers, onions, and sliced avocado. Roll up the burritos and serve with salsa on the side. Optionally, sprinkle cheese inside the burritos before rolling.

  • Zucchini and Carrot Muffins •

    Ingredients:Zucchini, carrots, whole wheat flour, eggs, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, honey, applesauce.

    Recipe: Grate zucchini and carrots, then mix with whole wheat flour, eggs, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, honey, and applesauce until combined. Pour the batter into muffin tins and bake until cooked through.

  • Sweet Potato Hash •

    Ingredients: Sweet potatoes, bell peppers, onion, garlic, spinach, olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika.

    Recipe: Dice sweet potatoes and saute with chopped bell peppers, onion, and garlic in olive oil until tender. Add spinach and cook until wilted. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.

  • Tomato and Basil Quiche •

    Ingredients: Pie crust, eggs, milk, tomatoes, fresh basil, onion, garlic, cheese (optional), salt, pepper.

    Recipe: Line a pie crust with sliced tomatoes, chopped fresh basil, sauteed onion, and garlic. Beat eggs with milk, salt, and pepper, then pour over the vegetables. Optionally, sprinkle cheese on top. Bake until the quiche is set and golden brown.

  • Cauliflower Breakfast Bowl •

    Ingredients: Cauliflower rice, kale, cherry tomatoes, avocado, eggs, olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika.

    Recipe: Saute cauliflower rice with chopped kale, halved cherry tomatoes, and sliced avocado in olive oil until heated through. Cook eggs to your liking and serve over the cauliflower mixture. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.

  • Broccoli and Cheese Muffins •

    Ingredients: Broccoli, eggs, whole wheat flour, baking powder, cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper.

    Recipe: Steam and chop broccoli, then mix with beaten eggs, whole wheat flour, baking powder, grated cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Pour the batter into muffin tins and bake until cooked through.

  • Overnight Oats •

    Ingredients: Rolled oats, milk (or yogurt), chia seeds, honey or maple syrup, fruits (such as berries, banana, or apple), nuts or seeds.

    Recipe: Mix rolled oats, milk (or yogurt), and chia seeds in a jar or bowl. Sweeten with honey or maple syrup if desired. Add fruits and nuts or seeds. Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning.

  • Quinoa Breakfast Bowl •

    Ingredients: Cooked quinoa, almond milk, cinnamon, vanilla extract, fruits (such as sliced banana, berries, or diced apple), nuts or seeds, honey or maple syrup.

    Recipe: Warm cooked quinoa with almond milk, cinnamon, and vanilla extract on the stove. Serve in a bowl and top with fruits, nuts or seeds, and a drizzle of honey or maple syrup.

  • Whole Grain Pancakes •

    Ingredients: Whole wheat flour, baking powder, salt, milk, eggs, vanilla extract, honey or maple syrup, fruits (such as blueberries or sliced banana).

    Recipe: Mix whole wheat flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl. In another bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, vanilla extract, and honey or maple syrup. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Cook pancakes on a griddle and serve with fruits.

  • Brown Rice Breakfast Porridge•

    Ingredients: Cooked brown rice, almond milk, cinnamon, nutmeg, honey or maple syrup, fruits (such as diced pear or peach), nuts or seeds.

    Recipe: Heat cooked brown rice with almond milk, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a saucepan until warm and creamy. Sweeten with honey or maple syrup. Serve in a bowl and top with fruits and nuts or seeds.

  • Whole Grain Toast with Nut Butter •

    Ingredients: Whole grain bread, nut butter (such as almond butter or peanut butter), banana slices, chia seeds or hemp seeds, honey or maple syrup.

    s Recipe: Toast whole grain bread. Spread nut butter on top. Arrange banana slices on top and sprinkle with chia seeds or hemp seeds. Drizzle with honey or maple syrup.

  • Barley Breakfast Bowl•

    Ingredients: Cooked barley, Greek yogurt, honey or maple syrup, fruits (such as sliced strawberries or kiwi), nuts or seeds (such as sliced almonds or pumpkin seeds).

    Recipe: Spoon cooked barley into a bowl. Top with Greek yogurt, fruits, and nuts or seeds. Drizzle with honey or maple syrup.

  • Whole Grain Muesli•

    Ingredients: Rolled oats, mixed nuts (such as almonds, walnuts, and pecans), dried fruits (such as raisins, cranberries, and apricots), seeds (such as sunflower seeds and pumpkin seeds), cinnamon, honey or maple syrup, yogurt or milk.

    Recipe: Mix rolled oats, nuts, dried fruits, seeds, and cinnamon in a bowl. Sweeten with honey or maple syrup. Serve with yogurt or milk.

  • Whole Grain Breakfast Cookies •

    Ingredients: Whole wheat flour, rolled oats, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, mashed banana, almond butter, honey or maple syrup, dried fruits (such as raisins or chopped dates), nuts or seeds.

    Recipe: Mix whole wheat flour, rolled oats, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon in a bowl. In another bowl, mix mashed banana, almond butter, and honey or maple syrup. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Fold in dried fruits and nuts or seeds. Form into cookies and bake until golden brown.
